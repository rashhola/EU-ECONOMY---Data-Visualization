Project Summary

The purpose of this project is to visualize the European Union's Economic Indicators. The visualizations will illustrate all current 27 Countries of the European Union based on the Official Website of the European Union. Economic indicators such as the Gross Domestic Product (GDP), Consumer Price Index (CPI), Unemployment Rates and Population are visualized for each country. The data for these indicators were obtained from The World Bank database.
